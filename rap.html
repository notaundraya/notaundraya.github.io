Rap Information

What's Rap?

Rapping is a musical form of vocal delivery that incorporates "rhyme, rhythmic speech, and street vernacular". It is performed or chanted, usually over a backing beat or musical accompaniment.

The components of rap include "content", "flow" (rhythm, rhyme), and "delivery". Rap differs from spoken-word poetry in that it is usually performed off-time to musical accompaniment.
